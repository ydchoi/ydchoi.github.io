Clinical machine learning group

I am a junior research scientist at CILVR/Facebook lab at NYU under the supervision of David Sontag. My research has focused on machine learning, with a particular concentration on applications of deep learning to clinical medicine. For my recent work, I have received an Honorable Mention in the CRA Outstanding Undergraduate Researcher Awards 2015, sponsored by Microsoft Research. Currently, I am interested in the mathematical analysis of convolutions, as I believe that the analysis is crucial for the extension of deep learning outside of standard regime, such as computer vision. Previously, I graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy, where I was a qualifier of USAMO in 2009. Most importantly, I am a student of Computer Science and Mathematics.
